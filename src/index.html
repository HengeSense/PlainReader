Welcome to PlainReader, a minimal web-based RSS client for NewsBlur.

Try it out in demo mode, log in with your NewsBlur account, or contribute on GitHub.

More info »

Warning: You're using an unsupported browser or device. PlainReader is designed to work on the latest WebKit-based desktop browsers, such as Chrome and Safari, and iPad running iOS 5+.

Go ahead and give it a try if you want, but I can't guarantee anything will look right or work properly!
